Post Tenebras Lux (Light After Darkness)

Soli Deo Gloria ! Aut quis prior dedit illi et retribuetur ei, Quoniam ex ipso, et per ipsum, et in ipso sunt omnia: ipsi gloria in saecula. Amen. (Rom 11:35-36)
Glory to God alone ! Or who hath first given to him, and it shall be recompensed unto him again? For of him, and through him, and to him, are all things: to whom be glory for ever. Amen. (Rom 11:35-36)
